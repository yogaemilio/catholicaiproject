Key Documents

Below are documents that I personally have found relevant in understanding what the Vatican has said about artificial intelligence. They are listed in chronological order, with the most recent at the top and older documents further down.

  • Message of His Holiness Pope Leo XIV for the 60th World Day of Social Communications (24 January 2026)
  • Antiqua et Nova: Note on the Relationship Between Artificial Intelligence and Human Intelligence (14 January 2025)
  • Address of Pope Francis to Participants in the International Conference of the Centesimus Annus Pro Pontifice Foundation (22 June 2024)
  • Address of His Holiness Pope Francis at the G7 Session on Artificial Intelligence (14 June 2024)
  • Message of His Holiness Pope Francis for the 58th World Day of Social Communications (24 January 2024)
  • Message of His Holiness Pope Francis for the 57th World Day of Peace (1 January 2024)
  • Address of His Holiness Pope Francis to Participants in the Minerva Dialogues (27 March 2023)
  • Address Prepared by Pope Francis for the Plenary Assembly of the Pontifical Academy for Life (read by Archbishop Paglia) (28 February 2020)
